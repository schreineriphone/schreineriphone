About us

A Few Words About Schreiner iPhone Repair

Schreiner iPhone Repair specializes in iPhone, iPad, and also MacBook repairs. We fix damaged screens, battery replacements, non-working charging ports, water damage and much more! This list is the most “common” repairs but anything apple related we could fix. We strive to have the best service at the lowest cost in the area; we are able to have this competitive pricing due to being a home business. Schreiner iPhone Repair guarantees their work 100% with a lifetime warranty. Feel free to contact me via phone call, or email with any questions you may have. We look forward to talking to you and helping you bring your electronic device back to life.

About Me

I was born and raised in Iowa. In 2012, I didn’t have the slightest clue how to do any repairs and thought it would be a good idea to tear into my brand new iPhone 5s. I was wanting to do a “back housing replacement” on it which would change the phone from a space gray color to a red iPhone; something nobody else really had at the time. As far as repairs go this repair would be considered one of the most difficult out there based on the soul factor of having to disassemble the entire iPhone. I didn’t have the right tools or skills to do the repair but I managed to do it without any problems. I thought to myself there could be some potential business with doing repairs. I first started doing repairs for my friends and family members and over the past four years the word has spread and my business has grown. To this day, I have only came across two repairs I could not fix (due to the phones being damaged beyond repair). I am currently a student at Iowa State University, perusing a degree in Exercise Science. I intend to enroll in Chiropractic school after I am done with my degree here in Ames.
